The best proviers for Office suite are Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace

Microsoft 365

  • Word, Powerpoint, Excel, OneNote
  • Outloook, Exchange, Teams
  • OneDrive, To Do, Power BI, and more
  • A lot of programs that make confusing prices

Google Workspace

  • Docs, Sheets, Slides, Cloud Search
  • Gmail, Drive, Meet, AppSheet
  • Chat, Calendar, Sites, and more
  • Straightforward prices
  • More file storage and also more email storage.

Conclusions

If you are concerned with cloud storage or email storage Google Workspace might be the best solution, apart from this it depends on what the company is used to working with and what platform you like more.
